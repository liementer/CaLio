# Privacy Policy for CaLio

**Last Updated: November 6, 2025**

## Overview

CaLio ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and stored when you use our calorie tracking application.

## Information We Collect

CaLio collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

### Food and Nutrition Data
- Food entries (name, calories, protein, carbs, fat, serving size)
- Meal types and timestamps
- Water intake entries

### Personal Profile Information
- User name (optional)
- Weight and height
- Age and gender
- Activity level
- Daily calorie and macronutrient targets

### Generated Statistics
- Daily calorie totals and macronutrient breakdowns
- Weekly progress and trends
- Streak information
- BMI and TDEE calculations

## How We Store Your Data

### Local Storage Only
- **All data is stored exclusively on your device** using Android's SharedPreferences
- **No data is transmitted to any external servers or cloud services**
- **No network connection is required or used** for any functionality
- Your data never leaves your device

### Data Security
- Data is stored in your device's private app storage
- Data is protected by your device's security measures
- Data is only accessible while the app is installed

## How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

1. **Tracking your calorie and nutrition intake**
2. **Calculating and displaying daily statistics**
3. **Generating progress charts and insights**
4. **Computing health metrics** (BMI, BMR, TDEE)
5. **Personalizing your experience** based on your goals and settings

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

- **We do not share your data with any third parties**
- **We do not collect any analytics or usage data**
- **We do not use any third-party tracking services**
- **We do not have access to your data** - it exists only on your device

## Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

### Access
- You can view all your data within the app at any time

### Modification
- You can edit or delete any food entries through the app interface
- You can update your profile settings at any time

### Deletion
- You can delete individual entries within the app
- **Complete data deletion**: Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data from your device

## Children's Privacy

CaLio does not knowingly collect or store information from children under the age of 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not require age verification. Parents and guardians should supervise children's use of the app.

## Changes to Data Storage

CaLio operates entirely offline with no external dependencies:
- **No user accounts** - no registration required
- **No cloud backup** - data is not synced across devices
- **No data export to external services**

If you reinstall the app or switch devices, your previous data will not be available unless you manually back up your device data.

## Medical Disclaimer

CaLio is designed for informational and tracking purposes only:
- **Not a substitute for professional medical advice**
- Consult healthcare professionals before making dietary changes
- BMI and TDEE calculations are estimates and may not be accurate for all individuals

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and this document with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device for as long as:
- The app remains installed on your device
- You do not manually delete entries

**Complete data removal** occurs when you uninstall the app from your device.

## No Advertisements or Marketing

- CaLio does not display advertisements
- We do not use your data for marketing purposes
- We do not send promotional communications

## Technical Implementation

For transparency, here's how your data is technically stored:

- **Storage Method**: Android SharedPreferences (private app storage)
- **Encryption**: Relies on your device's built-in security
- **Backup**: Only through Android's device backup (if enabled on your device)
- **Data Format**: Structured text stored locally
